Supply Work

We pride ourselves on not only offering top-notch fabrication services but also providing a comprehensive range of high-quality steel products to support your construction projects.

Our supply division is dedicated to offering a diverse selection of steel-related materials, ensuring that you have access to everything you need to complete your building construction efficiently and effectively. From structural components to finishing touches, we've got you covered.

Here's a glimpse of the steel materials we supply:
1. Steel Sheet Panels: Ideal for roofing and wall applications, our steel sheet panels are crafted with precision and durability to withstand harsh weather conditions while providing exceptional aesthetics.
2. H/I Beams: Engineered for structural integrity, our H/I beams offer superior strength and support, making them essential for a wide range of building construction projects.
3. Decking Panels (Bondeck XP): Our decking panels are designed to enhance the structural integrity of floors and roofs, providing a stable and reliable foundation for your building structures.
4. Steel Rafters: Built to withstand heavy loads and adverse environmental conditions, our steel rafters offer unmatched durability and longevity, ensuring the stability and safety of your buildings.
5. C-Purlins and Z-Purlins: These essential components provide structural support and stability for roofs and walls, enhancing the overall strength and resilience of your building framework.
6. Anchor Bolts: Ensuring secure anchoring of structural components, our anchor bolts are manufactured to meet stringent quality standards, providing peace of mind and reliability.
7. Structural Nut-Bolts and Fasteners (Screws): From assembling steel frames to securing panels, our range of structural nut-bolts and fasteners are engineered for optimal performance and ease of installation.
8. Insulation: Enhance the energy efficiency and comfort of your buildings with our high-quality insulation materials, designed to regulate temperature and minimize heat loss.

At Steeltech, we understand the importance of reliability, quality, and timely delivery. That's why we strive to maintain a robust inventory of steel materials and accessories, ensuring prompt fulfillment of your orders and seamless integration into your construction projects.

Erection Work

Our erection services encompass a comprehensive range of tasks, ensuring that every aspect of your building framework is assembled with precision and efficiency. Here's what you can expect from our erection team:

1. H Beam Erection (Column, Beam & Rafters): Our skilled erection team specializes in the precise installation of H beams, whether they're utilized as columns, beams, or rafters. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to structural integrity, we ensure that every H beam is positioned and secured according to exact specifications.

2. C & Z Purlin Installation: Purlins play a crucial role in providing structural support for roofs and walls. Our experienced erection team is proficient in the proper installation of C & Z purlins, ensuring optimal alignment and stability to support the overall framework of your building.

3. Hi-Tensile Industrial Profile Sheet Erection: We understand the importance of utilizing high-quality profile sheets for roofing and cladding applications. Our erection team is equipped with the expertise and tools necessary to handle the installation of Hi-Tensile Industrial Profile Sheets with precision and care, ensuring a seamless finish that enhances the durability and aesthetics of your building.

4. Translucent Sheets and Insulation Installation: In addition to structural components, we also specialize in the installation of translucent sheets and insulation materials to enhance the functionality and energy efficiency of your building. Our team ensures that every detail is meticulously executed, from properly sealing joints to optimizing insulation placement for maximum effectiveness.

At Steeltech, we prioritize efficiency, safety, and quality in every erection project we undertake. Our erection team comprises skilled professionals who are equipped with the latest machinery and tools to streamline the installation process while adhering to the highest standards of craftsmanship.

By choosing Steeltech Building Systems Limited for your erection needs, you can trust that your construction project will be in capable hands from start to finish. Contact us today to learn more about our erection services and how we can contribute to the success of your upcoming projects
